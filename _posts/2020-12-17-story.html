December, 17 2020

One day before I leave this special city. Suddenly a sweet and lovely girl gave me a gift and said thank you for what I have did to her. This simply but precious gift make me realized that everything we did to others matters.

Sometimes we don’t realized how precious our lives for others. We didn’t even realized that what we did become impact to others.

I am thankful for this meaningful live. Now I know that every little things I do is never wasted.
